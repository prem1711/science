B. Name the following-

  2. The nutrients that are also called body building foods : proteins
  3. A diet containing all the nutrients in right amount,along with roughage and water : balanced diet.
  4. The method of food preservation in which water is removed from the food : drying method.
  5. The method of food preservation in which fruits and vegetables are mixed with salt and oil : pickling method.

C. Short answer questions-

  1. What are nutrients?

    Ans) The food we eat contains substances that make us strong and healthy. These substances are called nutrient.

  2. Name the main nutrients found in our food.

    Ans) The five main nutrients found in our food are carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals.

  3. How does calcium help us? Name a calcium-rich food.

    Ans) Calcium is needed for the formation of bones and teeth. Milk, curd and cheese are rich sources of calcium.

  4. Why is roughage important?

    Ans) Roughage helps to remove waste materials from our body.

  5. Why should we drink enough water every day?

    Ans) Water Is an essential part of our diet. It is important for digestion and is an essential part of the blood and other body fluids.So, we must drink plenty of water every day.

  6. What is a balanced diet?

    Ans) A balanced diet is a diet that contains all the nutrient in right amount,along with roughage and water.

  7. When does food get spoilt?

    Ans) Food gets spoilt when it is not stored properly.

  8. What is the difference between refrigeration and deep freezing methods of food preservation?

    Ans) In refrigeration method, food is kept inside the refrigerator at a low temperature but in deep freezing method, food is kept inside the freezer at a very low temperature.
