C. Short answer questions-

  1. What are nutrients?

    Ans) The food we eat contains substances that make us strong and healthy. These substances are called nutrient.

  2. Name the main nutrients found in our food.

    Ans) The five main nutrients found in our food are carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals.

  3. How does calcium help us? Name a calcium-rich food.

    Ans) Calcium is needed for the formation of bones and teeth. Milk, curd and cheese are rich sources of calcium.

  4. Why is roughage important?

    Ans) Roughage helps to remove waste materials from our body.

  5. Why should we drink enough water every day?

    Ans) Water Is an essential part of our diet. It is important for digestion and is an essential part of the blood and other body fluids.So, we must drink plenty of water every day.

  6. What is a balanced diet?

    Ans) A balanced diet is a diet that contains all the nutrient in right amount,along with roughage and water.

  7. When does food get spoilt?

    Ans) Food gets spoilt when it is not stored properly.

  8. What is the difference between refrigeration and deep freezing methods of food preservation?

    Ans) In refrigeration method, food is kept inside the refrigerator at a low temperature but in deep freezing method, food is kept inside the freezer at a very low temperature.

D. Long answer questions-

  1. What is the main function of carbohydrates?

    Ans) Carbohydrates provide energy to our body. Hence, food items rich in carbohydrates are called energy-giving foods.

  2. How do proteins help us? Why should young children include more protein-rich foods in their diet?

    Ans) Proteins are the nutrients that help in the growth and repair of our body. They also help in buildng our muscles. Young children should include more protein rich foods in their diet because their bodies are still growing.

  3. How do fats help us? Name two rich sources of fats.

    Ans) Fats are the nutrients that provide more energy to our body than carbohydrates. They help to keep our body warm. Milk Oil and nuts are examples of fat-rich foods.

  4. How do vitamins and minerals help us? Name two rich sources of vitamins and minerals.

    Ans) Vitamins and minerals are the nutrients that protects our body from diseases and keep us fit and healthy. The source of vitamin are green vegetable and source of minerals are milk and spinach.

  5. Explain any three methods to preserve food.

    Ans) Pickling,in this method. fruits and vegetables are mixed with salt and oil. An example is the pickling of mango and garlic.

    Drying,in this method, water is removed from the food. The food is then kept in an airtight container. An example is the drying of chillies in the sun.

    Canning and bottling,in this method. food is stored in tin cans and glass bottles. Examples are the canning of fish and bottling of jams.

  6. Write three steps to avoid wastage of food.

    Ans) 1. Avoid cooking a large quantity of food to avoid wastage.

    2. Cooked food should always be covered with a lid.

    3. Store cooked food in the refrigerator to avoid spoilage.
